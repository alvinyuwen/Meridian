Methodology & Limitations

  • Predicts direction (up/down) over a —-day horizon using insider Form 4 activity + basic price features.
  • Trained on a small set of tickers — results are illustrative, not statistically robust.
  • Backtest excludes transaction costs and slippage.
  • Time-based train/test split (no shuffling) to avoid look-ahead bias; labels use only future prices, features use only past data as of each date.
